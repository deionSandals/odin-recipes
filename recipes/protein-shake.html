Trent's Protein Shake Recipe

Protein shake with no filler carbs or unhealthy fats

Ingredients

  • Hydrolized Whey Protein - 20g
  • Water, Almond Milk, or Milk - 8oz
  • Oat Powder - 30g
  • Almond Flour(powder) - 15g
  • Ground Chia Seed - 15g

Steps

  1. Pour water or other into glass
  2. Dump all other ingredients in
  3. Stir or blend
